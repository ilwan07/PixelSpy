What is PixelSpy?

With this platform, you can monitor the mails you send, and know when someone opens a mail you sent them.

How does this work?

To achieve that, this platform will give you the link to an image that you need to add at the end of your mails. This image is a transparent 1x1 image which is practically invisible to the receiver. When the person opens the mail, a request is automatically made by his mail client to our servers to retrieve the invisible image, but thanks to this request we know that the mail has been opened, and from which device.

Who can I send tracked mails to?

Make sure that the person you're going to send tracked mails to is aware of it, and has accepted it. Never put a tracker in a mail if the receiver doesn't know it, as it may constitute a privacy violation.
